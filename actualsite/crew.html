The Original Three

From left to right, Gavin Bower, "Big Head" Beghetti, and Kiera Namaste have a combined experience of over 38 years. Gavin, who was originally working as an engineer at Boeing one day questioned why should we spend so much time and effort on airplanes that require such extensive pre-travel preparations when it all could be focused on personal air travel. This was achieved with the help of Big head, who worked closely with Gavin at Boeing, and Kiera, the youngest of the three they met while working internationally in Japan. Kiera is known for her intricate mechanic skills and was a large part in early development. The humble beginnings all started when Gavin decided to build a team to develop something great, something that has become bigger than themselves, and something that has change the world by storm.
