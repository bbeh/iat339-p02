Standard Jetpack Features

We don't compromise on safety. This is why all jetpacks no matter the price all come loaded with the newest and most innovative safety features.

False-Start Technology

False-start prevents your Jetpack from accelerating until the back brace is securely fastened onto the pilot. Using innovative sensors and technology even a loose clip will prevent the Jetpack from thrusting into the air leaving you behind on the ground.

 

Safe and Secure Back Brace

Patent Pending technology our Jetpacks features the most comfortable back brace available. Allowing you to accelerate with comfort and trust that the Jetpack won't take off without you. It also features False-Start to keep you safe.

Turbo-charged Thrusters

Packing over 700 HP these thrusters will ensure you have full control even in the toughest windstorms*. Our turbo-charged thrusters also utilize clean burning diesel fuel to keep the atmosphere happy while you're zooming around.

*For your safety we do not recommend operating a Jetpack in winds exceeding 100 km/H.

Auto Guidance Wings

Don't ever worry about flying off course. Our auto guidance wings will take you where you need to go without much effort. Simply program in the destination prior to take off and let auto guidance take you there safely.
